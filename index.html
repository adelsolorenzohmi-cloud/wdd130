Guatemala

Located in the heart of Guatemala's jungle, Tikal is one of the largest and most significant archaeological sites of the ancient Maya civilization. It was a major urban and ceremonial center from the 6th century B.C. to the 10th century A.D. The site is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, celebrated for its impressive temples, pyramids, and palaces.
